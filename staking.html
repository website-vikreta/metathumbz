In-Game Players

The first-gen MetaThumbz NFTs will be in-game players for our multiple P2E games and NFT holders will have some benefits like Free or Discounted Entry in Tournaments and early access to all upcoming features. The first-gen NFT holders will be a first-class citizen of the Metaverse.

Passive Income

Earn $RING tokens by staking your NFTs. $RING tokens will be used for several exciting activities in the MetaThumbz ecosystem like Participating in tournaments, Customizing & Upgrading, Trading in Marketplaze, etc. In the future, the tokens might give access to other exclusive experiences, items, perks, content and more within the MetaThumbz ecosystem.

Exclusive Community

Be a member of an exclusive community of first-gen Thumbz and get a chance to vote in all decisions for the metaverse. In the exclusive community, you can read announcements about the MetaThumbz, Jump in discussions about the overall development of the ecosystem, Play community-based games, or join giveaways.

Royalty

75% Secondary Royalty will go to Minters and 25% Secondary Royalty will be used to buy back & burn Ring tokens as well as Sweep the floor.

Profit Sharing

All the NFT Holders will receive a percentage from the fees collected on all transactions in the metaverse. In the future, we plan to create a shared treasury where all the proceeds from Secondary Sales & Fees will be collected and will be governed by ThumbzDAO. We will also start burning a percentage of the supply of $RING whenever it is used. The exact terms are TBD with the community.

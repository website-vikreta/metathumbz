How
To
Buy

  • Buy Solana

    1. Signup on a cryptocurrency exchange like Binance, Kucoin, WazirX (for India), FTX, Huobi or any other of your choice.
    2. Transfer Fiat currency like USD, Euro, INR, etc. to the exchange.
    3. Buy SOLANA (1.5 recommended considering the Mint price and fees)

  • Create a Phantom Wallet

    1. Visit - https://phantom.app/
    2. Click on Download or Add to Chrome
    3. Install the Phantom Wallet

  • Fund your Phantom Wallet

    1. Copy your wallet address from Phantom
    2. Transfer the purchased SOLANA from the exchange

  • Mint on Sale day

    1. Visit the Mint section of the website
    2. Choose the number of Thumbz to mint (max 20 per wallet)
    3. Approve the transaction on Phantom App
